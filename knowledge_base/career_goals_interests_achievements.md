## Behavioral / STAR — Biggest Challenge & Conflict
*(Startup example.)* **Situation:** During the startup, my team came from different backgrounds, and some partners weren't technical but had many ideas about how things should be implemented. **Task:** I needed to respect their input while helping them understand which requirements were realistic and which weren't feasible. **Action:** Instead of dismissing ideas, I used business language, demos, prototypes, and cost analysis to explain the trade-offs of each option—translating technical concepts into business terms they could understand. **Result:** The team converged on a single plan, the product launched successfully, and I gradually took on the technical-lead role. My biggest takeaway: the hard part isn't writing code—it's communication, collaboration, driving a project forward, and balancing technology with business. It was the period in which I grew the fastest.
